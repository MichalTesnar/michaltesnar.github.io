Hey, everyone. Hope you are having a splendid weekend! A lot has happened since I was last writing here, and it is tough to be able to desribe everything that I am thinking about!

I spent an amazing weekend spend with my friend Olek who visited me from Netherlands, and went to make Chocolate in Lindt.

{% include figure.liquid loading="eager" path="assets/img/blog/chocolate.jpeg" class="img-fluid rounded z-depth-1 mx-auto d-block" width="400" height="200" alt="Chocolate is good." %}

We had a wonderful time revisiting nostalgically the times we had back in Groningen, and just discussing how we view life, professional and personal, as of now. I feel extremely grateful for these precious moments!

During his visit, we took a short swing by a Czechoslovak party, where I met these beautiful people.

{% include figure.liquid loading="eager" path="assets/img/blog/blog_lovers.jpg" class="img-fluid rounded z-depth-1 mx-auto d-block" width="700" height="200" alt="My dear readers." %}

You might be thinking, why are you even showing this photo to us? Well, so it happens that among them, there is one reader of my blog! (Spot him if you can!) That has absolutely stunned me, never would have thought that any of my peers would ever look at my posts unless I forced them to. Honestly, it made me feel a bit awkward, and rethink what I am sharing, and if I actually would like to have a bigger audience. I came to the following conclusion:

1. I am not yet fully sure what I am doing with my blog, what audience I want to reach and why,
2. hence I feel weird about somebody reading it,
3. maybe in the future I will give this more shape and I will have a target audience,
4. if (3) happens, I think then will have authentic message to share,
5. if (4) happens, I hope I will identify with that message enough to be able to represent it.

So as of current, I am not a famous blogger, and I am not gonna become famous, cause even if I wanted, I actually do not know if I could, cause I would not want to. Maybe it will come with time. Till then this is undercover blog. Read it before others find it to be cool :)

Thanks for reading!

Talk to you soon! :)
